Safety & health

Trek Insurance Check

free check

Heli-rescue covered?

We read your policy's fine print for the two clauses that matter in Nepal: helicopter evacuation and altitude ceiling (most cap at 4,000 m, EBC needs 6,000 m). No policy? We'll point you to three that work.

15-min telehealth

A CIWEC-trained travel doctor reviews your meds, history and itinerary and advises on altitude medication like Diamox where appropriate, before you're at 4,000 m wishing you had. Any prescription stays the clinician's call.
