Thamel heritage walk

Thamel and old Kathmandu

Step out of the souvenir strip into the courtyards behind it: hidden stupas, a fifteenth century bahal, and the Asan morning market where Kathmandu actually shops.

Ten metres behind Thamel's shopfronts the old city keeps its own hours. A host from the neighbourhood walks you through carved courtyards the guidebooks skip, past a stupa that predates the tourist quarter by five centuries, and into the spice-and-marigold crush of Asan's morning market. You end with milk tea where the porters take theirs, knowing how to read a bahal doorway for the rest of your trip.

  • 2.5 hours
  • Basecamp Thamel courtyard
  • Year-round
  • any fitness

What this is not

This is not a shopping tour: the point is the ten metres behind the shopfronts, where the old city keeps its own hours.

What's included

  • Local host from the neighbourhood
  • Asan and Indra Chowk morning market
  • Three hidden bahals and a living stupa
  • Milk tea stop, on us

Good to know. Mornings are best: the market peaks before nine and the courtyards stay quiet. Wear shoes you trust on worn brick.
