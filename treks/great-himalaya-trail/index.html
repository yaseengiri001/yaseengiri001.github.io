New

The full traverse of Nepal, Kanchenjunga in the east to Api in the west, joined along the highest walkable line. Crossings like the Lumba Sumba pass go days between villages.

What this is not

This is not a holiday: it is a five month commitment that most people section-hike across several years.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Itinerary

Full day-by-day plan prepared on inquiry

We publish stage-by-stage plans only after a route specialist has walked the current conditions: seasons move teahouses, roads and river crossings on this route. The logistics above are real; the daily plan is built for your dates.

Safety, plainly

Remoteness is the risk: the high route crosses passes above 6,000 m, days from any road, and evacuation depends on weather windows and insurance that genuinely covers them. Resupply is planned district by district.

Fitness, honestly

Months of full walking days back to back: proven multi-week trekking experience, sound joints and a body that recovers overnight are the entry requirements.
