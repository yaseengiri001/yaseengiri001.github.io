New

A quiet crossing between the Langtang and Ganesh Himal ranges, from Gatlang's stone Tamang houses to the Gurung villages of Tipling and Shertung. The valley takes its name from the rubies once mined near Somdang, below the Pangsang Pass.

What this is not

The lodges here are family homes with shared rooms and whatever the household is cooking: warm, but not hotels.

Safety, plainly

Health posts between Gatlang and Dhading Besi are basic and phone signal is patchy: carry your personal medication. Cloud builds over the Pangsang Pass by late morning, so we cross early.

Fitness, honestly

Five to six hour days with one long pull up to the pass; a walker who is comfortable on steep hill paths will manage.
