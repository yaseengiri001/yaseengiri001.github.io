New

Nepal's lowest glacier lake, a green pool in a stone cirque where avalanches off Annapurna II rumble across the water all afternoon. You walk in from Sikles on herders' trails along the Madi Khola.

What this is not

This is not a polished lodge route: beyond Sikles the stops are herders' shelters turned simple teahouses, and landslide scars cross the trail.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

Watch the avalanche fans from the viewpoint above the lake, never from the shoreline beneath them, and expect the upper trail to shift a little after every monsoon.

Fitness, honestly

Moderate: valley walking rather than big climbs, but the days are long and the ground uneven. Sure footing matters more than lungs here.
