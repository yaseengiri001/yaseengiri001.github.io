New

Five lakes in a stone bowl at the head of the Indrawati river: a pilgrimage site where jhankri shamans drum through the Janai Purnima full moon each August. For the rest of the year you may have the shrine and its prayer flags to yourselves.

What this is not

This is not a lodge trek: beyond the last village the shelters are bare pilgrim huts, so the honest way to walk it is supported camping with tents and a cook.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

You gain around 2,500 m from the roadhead in three days, and storms build quickly off the Jugal peaks: keep a spare day in hand, and descend if altitude symptoms worsen overnight.

Fitness, honestly

Six to seven hour days on rough, lightly maintained trails with steady height gain; best for walkers with a previous trek behind them.
