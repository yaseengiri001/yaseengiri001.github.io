New

Kathmandu's forest summit, an hour from the ring road. Climb past the Nagi Gumba nunnery to Baghdwar, where the Bagmati begins at a stone tiger's mouth, then take the stepped summit early, before the haze rises.

What this is not

This is not a viewpoint peak: the summit sits in old oak and rhododendron, and the reward is birdsong, spring water and wild forest pressed against a capital city.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

Most of the climb is stone staircase, harder on the knees coming down, and in monsoon the leeches are relentless. Carry water; the army post at the gate logs walkers in and out.

Fitness, honestly

A sustained 1,200 m of ascent split across the overnight; steady walkers need no training.
