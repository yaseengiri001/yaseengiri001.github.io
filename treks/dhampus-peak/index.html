New

Thapa Peak on the climbing permit: a snow dome above French Pass where Dhaulagiri fills the western sky. Below the climb lies Hidden Valley, one of the emptiest high places in Nepal.

What this is not

This is not a gentle add-on to a Dhaulagiri trek: the dome is glaciated, the wind above French Pass rarely stops, and you climb roped behind an expedition team.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Full day-by-day plan prepared on inquiry

We publish stage-by-stage plans only after a route specialist has walked the current conditions: seasons move teahouses, roads and river crossings on this route. The logistics above are real; the daily plan is built for your dates.

Safety, plainly

Wind is the signature hazard: the ridge above French Pass funnels it, and summit-morning wind chill can freeze exposed skin in minutes. The dome's crevasses are roped terrain.

Fitness, honestly

Prior trekking above 5,000 m expected; the approach days are long and the summit push takes eight to ten hours.
