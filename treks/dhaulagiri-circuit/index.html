New

A full camping expedition around the seventh-highest mountain on earth: up the Myagdi Khola gorge to Dhaulagiri Base Camp, over French Pass into the Hidden Valley, and down to Marpha. No villages for a week, just glacier, moraine and canvas.

What this is not

This is not a trek with a safety margin: it is the hardest non-climbing route in this catalogue, with glacier travel, high camps and no lodges or exit roads for days.

Itinerary

Full day-by-day plan prepared on inquiry

We publish stage-by-stage plans only after a route specialist has walked the current conditions: seasons move teahouses, roads and river crossings on this route. The logistics above are real; the daily plan is built for your dates.

Safety, plainly

You sleep above five thousand metres in the Hidden Valley and cross two glaciated passes; the crew carries ropes, a satellite phone and a full medical kit. Weather can pin the team down, so the itinerary holds spare days.

Fitness, honestly

The fittest trekkers find this hard: long days on moraine and snow, then cold nights under canvas. Previous experience on a camping trek at altitude is strongly advised.
