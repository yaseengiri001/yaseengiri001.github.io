New

A short walk into big country: up the Suli Gaad gorge to Phoksundo, Nepal's deepest lake, turquoise under bare cliffs beside the Bon village of Ringmo. Acclimatised parties can extend over the Numa La towards Do Tarap.

What this is not

This is not a comfort trek: lodges beyond Chhepka are basic, with mud floors, shared rooms and no heating beyond the kitchen stove.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

Flights out of Juphal are morning-only and frequently lost to wind, so the itinerary keeps a spare day. The restricted permit requires a registered guide and a minimum party of two, both arranged for you.

Fitness, honestly

Steady five to six hour days up a river gorge with one long climb to the lake shelf; a fit hill walker will manage.
