New

The gentlest introduction on this list: a walk-up glacier summit above Kyanjin Gompa at the head of the Langtang valley. From the top, Shishapangma stands across the border in Tibet.

What this is not

This is the honest exception on the list, a true walk-up, but it still crosses a glacier: you go roped, in crampons, with a climbing partner, not alone in trainers.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Full day-by-day plan prepared on inquiry

We publish stage-by-stage plans only after a route specialist has walked the current conditions: seasons move teahouses, roads and river crossings on this route. The logistics above are real; the daily plan is built for your dates.

Safety, plainly

Even a gentle glacier hides crevasses under spring snow, and the summit morning is well below freezing; rope up, and acclimatise on the valley walk first.

Fitness, honestly

The most approachable climb here: hill fitness and one previous trek near 4,000 m are enough, with a six to eight hour summit day.
