The valley of glaciers, a day's drive from Kathmandu: yak cheese, Tamang heritage, and the 360° summit of Kyanjin Ri.

Langtang is the comeback story of Nepali trekking. Rebuilt with extraordinary spirit after 2015, its lodges are warm, its people are glad you came, and its upper valley, a U-shaped glacial corridor under Langtang Lirung, is as dramatic as anywhere in the Khumbu at half the cost and crowds. Climb Kyanjin Ri at dawn, tour the 300-year-old cheese factory, and walk out wishing you'd added two days.

Highlights

Why this one

  • Kyanjin Ri (4,773 m): a real summit, no ropes
  • The ancient yak-cheese factory at Kyanjin Gompa
  • Tamang villages and water-driven prayer wheels
  • Every rupee here rebuilds the valley
  • Closest big trek to Kathmandu: one drive, no flight

Acclimatisation

The altitude story

Sleep altitudes, day by day. The dips are deliberate: climb high, sleep low is how your blood learns thin air.

Acclimatisation check: the steepest sleep gain is +1050 m (day 3): above 500 m, so the itinerary builds in an acclimatisation day around it.

Itinerary

Day by day

1

Drive Kathmandu → Syabrubesi

sleep 1,550 m

7–8 hours over the Trisuli gorge roads. Sleep early; the trail starts at the bridge.

2

Syabrubesi → Lama Hotel

11 km · 6 h · sleep 2,380 m

Riverside forest all day: langurs, honey hives on cliffs, one steady climb.

3

Lama Hotel → Langtang Village

15 km · 6–7 h · sleep 3,430 m

You pass the memorial to the old village, walk it quietly, and arrive into the rebuilt one, brighter than ever.

4

Langtang → Kyanjin Gompa

7 km · 3–4 h · sleep 3,870 m

Short day past mani walls longer than football pitches. Afternoon: monastery, bakery, cheese factory.

5

Kyanjin Ri sunrise, rest day

6 km · 5 h · sleep 3,870 m

Up at 4:30 for the ridge: Langtang Lirung's icefalls glow pink across the valley. Down for pancakes.

6

Kyanjin → Lama Hotel

19 km · 7 h · sleep 2,380 m

Long, lovely descent: the valley replays itself in reverse.

7

Walk out & drive to Kathmandu

11 km · 4 h + drive · sleep 1,400 m

Morning walk to Syabrubesi, jeep home, dinner in Thamel.

Safety, plainly

The jump from Lama Hotel to Langtang Village is the big altitude day. Hydrate and go slow. Kyanjin Ri is optional and weather-dependent; your guide decides at dawn.
