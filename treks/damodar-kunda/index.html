New

Beyond the villages of Upper Mustang, three sacred lakes lie in a bowl of scree where Hindu pilgrims gather at Janai Purnima each August. The approach passes the cave monastery of Luri Gompa and some of the emptiest country in Nepal.

What this is not

This is not a teahouse route: beyond Luri Gompa there are no lodges, no shops and no phone signal, only what the mule train carries.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

High camps are cold even in midsummer and the nearest help is days away: acclimatisation is written into the route, not optional. The restricted permit requires a registered guide and full camping crew, both included.

Fitness, honestly

Long walking days at altitude on rough ground; previous trekking above four thousand metres matters more than gym fitness.
