New

The sacred milk lake below Numbur, the peak Solu Sherpas call their valley god, reached through yak pastures above Phaplu. At the August full moon of Janai Purnima, pilgrims come to bathe; most other weeks the shore is yours.

What this is not

This is not polished teahouse country: the highest lodges are seasonal herders' shelters, and mist can sit on the lake for a day at a time.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

You sleep well above 4,000 metres near the lake after a fast rise from the valley; keep the rest day, and treat a worsening headache as a reason to descend.

Fitness, honestly

Steady mid-hills walking with one long push to the lake; fine for a regular hill walker.
