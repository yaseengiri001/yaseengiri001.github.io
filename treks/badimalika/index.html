New

In the monsoon shoulder the meadows stand ankle-deep in flowers below the hilltop shrine of Malika Bhagwati, and on the August full moon the Janai Purnima fair brings lines of pilgrims up from Martadi. Outside the fair the ridges belong to shepherds.

What this is not

This is not a stroll between lodges: water points sit hours apart on the high meadows, and phone signal drops out a day above Martadi.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

Storms build over the open meadows by early afternoon in the fair season, and the only shelter is a shepherd hut, so ridge days start at first light. Evacuation is a weather-dependent helicopter from Nepalgunj.

Fitness, honestly

Demanding: long ridge days with two steep pulls near the shrine, and the thin air slows everyone. Train with full-day hikes beforehand.
