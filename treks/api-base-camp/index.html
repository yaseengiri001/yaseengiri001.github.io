New

The far west's highest mountain rises in one white wall above a valley of stone-roofed villages and walnut trees. You follow the Chameliya river from Ghusa to a base camp meadow beside the small lake of Kali Dhunga.

What this is not

This is not a fly-in trek: reaching the roadhead costs a long jeep day beyond Dhangadhi airport, often two, and the same again on the way home.

The route's honest rules1 facts · dated

Guide rule: national mandateas of 2023-04

Since April 2023 foreign trekkers in national parks need a licensed guide via a registered agency. Yours is included.

Safety, plainly

There is no rescue post beyond Gokuleshwar, and the upper Chameliya trail crosses landslide slopes that shift each monsoon. Helicopter evacuation stages from Dhangadhi and waits on weather, so insurance with heli cover is required.

Fitness, honestly

Six to seven hour days on rough trail with steady altitude gain to base camp; you need to be comfortable camping and walking on consecutive days.
