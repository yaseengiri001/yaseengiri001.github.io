Privacy

Your data, in plain language.

A product whose whole pitch is trust owes you a readable privacy notice. Here is what this demo does today, and how the live product is designed to handle the sensitive things.

What this demo actually collects

This is a demo build. It does not take real payments, passports, or live GPS. In plain terms, what touches a server versus what stays on your device:

  • Account: if you sign in, an email address and authentication token are handled by our auth provider (Supabase) to keep you signed in.
  • On your device only: your saved places, trip drafts, AI plans, packing progress and chosen mode live in your browser's local storage, not on our servers. Clearing your browser clears them.
  • Simulated: bookings, payments, the document vault, family location share and SOS are interface demonstrations. No real document, card, or location is captured.

Documents (passports, permits, insurance)

In the live product the document vault would hold identity-grade data, so it gets identity-grade handling: encrypted at rest with per-user keys and in transit, strict access control with audit logging, a defined retention window with auto-deletion, and a breach-notification commitment. In this demo, no real document is uploaded or stored.

Location and family share

Real-time location is sensitive. In the live product, family live-share would be opt-in per trip, on expiring and revocable links, visible only to people you authorise, at the minimum precision needed. In this demo the share page is illustrative and broadcasts no real location.

Health and altitude data

Altitude and medical information is special-category data. The live product would collect it only with explicit consent, handle it separately from the rest of your profile, and keep any clinical interaction with the licensed clinician. The planner's acclimatisation guidance is general information, not medical advice.

Translation and AI

Text you run through the translator or planner is processed to give you a result. The live product commits to disclosing any processors, limiting retention, and a no-training-on-your-content stance with our AI and translation vendors. This demo's AI runs on a deterministic local engine when no server key is present.

Payments

The live product would never store full card numbers: payments would be tokenised through a licensed payment provider (PCI scope stays with them). This demo processes no real payment of any kind.

Your rights

In the live product you can request a copy of your data, correct it, or have it deleted, and withdraw consent for sensitive processing at any time. Because this demo keeps your activity in your own browser, you are already in control: clearing site data removes it. For an account, sign out and request deletion via the contact below.
