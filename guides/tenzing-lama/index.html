“People come for Langtang Lirung. They come back for the people under it.”

Tenzing rebuilt his family lodge after 2015 and then went back to guiding because, he says, the valley heals fastest when it's walked. He reads the gompa murals like newspapers and pours the best butter tea on the trail. Choose him if you want the why of Langtang, not just the where.
