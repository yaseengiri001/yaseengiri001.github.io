Altitude, the four rules

1

Above 3,000 m, raise sleep altitude ≤ 500 m/day

The single biggest predictor of summiting happy.

2

Climb high, sleep low

Day-hike above your lodge, descend to sleep: it's why our profiles look like saw teeth.

3

3–4 litres of water a day

Altitude headaches are dehydration headaches half the time.

4

Tell your guide at the first symptom

A rest day at 3,500 m beats a helicopter at 4,800 m. Every time.
