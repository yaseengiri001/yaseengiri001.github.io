Street food that won't hurt you

The rules: hot, busy, fresh oil. Everything below passes all three.

Momo, steamed firstरू 120–200

Steam kills doubt. Graduate to fried once your gut has a Nepali visa.

Daal bhaat setरू 150–250

Refills included by sacred custom. Follow the office crowd at noon.

Sel roti + chiyaरू 60–100

Rice-ring doughnut, sweet milk tea, breakfast outside any temple.
